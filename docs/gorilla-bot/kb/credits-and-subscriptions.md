# Credits and Subscriptions

Parlay Gorilla offers free usage, premium subscriptions, and credits for pay-per-use access.

Subscriptions:
- Premium plans unlock higher usage limits and advanced tools.
- Subscription status and renewal info appear in the Profile and Billing pages.
- If you cancel, access continues until the current billing period ends.

Credits:
- Credits can be purchased in packs.
- Credits can be used when free or premium limits are exhausted.
- Credit balances are shown in the app and are deducted per AI action.

Pay-per-use:
- Single-use purchases may be offered for certain parlay types.
- Prices can differ between single-sport and multi-sport requests.

Important:
- Email verification is required before purchases in most cases.
- Limits and pricing can change by plan. Check the Billing page for current details.
