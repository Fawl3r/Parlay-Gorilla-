## Version 3: Comprehensive Business Description (Full)
**Best for:** About page, investor pitch deck, business plan

```
Parlay Gorilla is an AI-powered sports betting assistant that transforms how bettors approach parlay betting through data-driven insights and intelligent analysis. Founded to address the gap between casual betting and professional-grade analytics, our platform empowers users to build smarter, more informed parlay bets across multiple sports.

**Our Mission:**
To democratize professional-grade sports betting analytics, making data-driven decision-making accessible to all bettors, from casual fans to serious analysts.

**What We Do:**
Parlay Gorilla combines proprietary AI technology with real-time odds integration to help users build intelligent parlay bets (1-20 legs) across NFL, NBA, NHL, MLB, College Sports, Soccer, UFC, and Boxing. Our platform provides:

- **AI-Generated Parlay Picks**: Our proprietary AI engine analyzes games and generates ready-to-bet parlays with win probabilities, confidence scores, and detailed explanations for each pick
- **Custom Parlay Builder**: Users can build their own parlays and receive AI-powered analysis showing combined win probability, expected value, and risk assessment
- **Game Analysis Hub**: Detailed matchup analysis with model win probabilities, ATS trends, over/under insights, and injury reports
- **Real-Time Odds Integration**: We aggregate live odds from all major sportsbooks to help users find the best value
- **Multi-Sport Coverage**: Comprehensive support for NFL, NBA, NHL, MLB, College Football, College Basketball, Soccer (EPL, MLS, La Liga), UFC, and Boxing

**Our Technology:**
Built on a modern tech stack (FastAPI, Next.js, PostgreSQL), Parlay Gorilla leverages machine learning algorithms, real-time data feeds, and natural language processing to deliver actionable insights. Our platform includes on-chain proof anchoring for parlay verification and a comprehensive analytics dashboard for tracking performance.

**Target Market:**
We serve sports bettors, fantasy sports players, and analytical bettors who value data-driven decision-making over random picks. Our user base includes both casual bettors looking to improve their success rate and serious analysts seeking professional-grade tools.

**Business Model:**
Parlay Gorilla operates on a freemium model with premium subscriptions ($39.99/month, $399.99/year, or $500 lifetime) and credit packs ($9.99-$59.99) for pay-per-use access. Premium features include 200 AI parlays per 30-day period, unlimited custom parlay builder access, upset finder tools, and comprehensive analytics.

**Our Differentiators:**
- **Transparency**: Unlike tipster services, we show actual win probabilities and expected value calculations
- **AI-Powered**: Proprietary algorithms analyze multiple data points to generate intelligent picks
- **Multi-Sport**: Comprehensive coverage across all major sports, not just NFL
- **Real-Time**: Live odds integration and up-to-the-minute game analysis
- **Educational**: We teach users WHY picks are strong, not just WHAT to bet

**Vision:**
To become the leading AI-powered sports betting assistant, helping millions of bettors make smarter, more profitable decisions through data-driven insights and intelligent analysis.
```
